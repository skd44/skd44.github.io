The Trip

Our camp would be situated far from all the city lights amidst mother nature and away from all the addictive gadgets. Customers can opt for our 1Day trip or 2Days trip packages depending on their requirements. The 1 day hiking trip would begin early in the morning, hiking our way to our camping site, where attendees would be given refreshments and then the activities would begin. Lunch would be provided. On the other hand, our 2Days trip is what customers should look out for since it has way many added activities and thrilling events. Night hiking and Camp Fire musical nights would be an added bonus for our excited attendees. Camping in tents surrounded by a warm camp fire away from all the hustle and bustle of the city will surely be a night one can never forget , and not to forget the soothing music our musical band would perform live. Couples are surely going to enjoy this one of a kind event . Our specialised photography team would make sure to capture all the priceless moments of our attendees and not to forget our skilled medical and safety team who would always be with us in case of any mishappenings. We are also planning to add the already installed ropeway in our package.
